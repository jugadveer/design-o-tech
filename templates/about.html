What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment analysis uses artificial intelligence to automatically detect emotions and opinions in text. Our advanced algorithms can understand context, sarcasm, and nuanced language to provide accurate sentiment classification.

Real Example:
"The product arrived late but works fine"
→ Neutral sentiment (logistics issue, not product quality)
